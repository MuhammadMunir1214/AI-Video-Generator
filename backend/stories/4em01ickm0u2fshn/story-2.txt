Leqembi's rollout is gradual due to the complex diagnosis process for early Alzheimer's. Tracey Collins took 2.5 years to get diagnosed. Finding a neurologist is tough, with long wait times. Only 24% of patients with neurological conditions see a neurologist. After diagnosis, patients undergo PET or spinal fluid tests to check for amyloid plaques. MRI scans ensure no other brain diseases. Some hospitals require genetic testing for the APOE4 gene variant. A panel of experts then decides eligibility. Insurance issues can delay treatment, especially for those not covered by Medicare.
